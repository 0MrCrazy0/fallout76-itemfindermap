# Fallout 76 Item Finder Map

**Live version:** [https://0mrcrazy0.github.io/fallout76-itemfindermap/](https://0mrcrazy0.github.io/fallout76-itemfindermap/)

**This is a free, fan-made tool for the Fallout 76 community. Not affiliated with Bethesda Softworks.**

A free, no-login browser tool for Fallout 76 players to mark and track items, locations, and discoveries across Appalachia.

## Features
- Right-click (PC) or long-press (mobile) anywhere on the map to create a **permanent marker** or **temporary postcard**
- Permanent markers are automatically locked for safety and can be unlocked and dragged to new positions
- Temporary postcards generate shareable links that expire after 5 minutes
- Powerful search and filter system (description, category, or grid coordinate)
- Export / Import your entire collection with one click
- Community Map system — download verified markers submitted by other players
- XP and level-up system that rewards exploration
- Full offline support after first load
- Progressive Web App (PWA) support for true fullscreen experience on mobile, pc, ios
- Ultra-wide screen support for pc

## How to Use
1. Open the [live map](https://0mrcrazy0.github.io/fallout76-itemfindermap/)
2. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) anywhere on the map
3. Choose **Create Permanent Marker** or **Create Temporary Postcard**
4. Use the search bar and tools panel to manage, filter, and organize your markers

Enjoy exploring Appalachia, Vault Dweller.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I create a marker?**  
A: Right-click (PC) or long-press (mobile) anywhere on the map and select “Create Permanent Marker”.

**Q: What is the difference between a permanent marker and a postcard?**  
A: Permanent markers stay on your map forever and can be moved, edited, or deleted. Postcards create a temporary shareable link that expires after 5 minutes — perfect for sharing with friends.

**Q: Why are some markers glowing green?**  
A: Newly created or recently moved markers glow for 2 minutes as a visual indicator.

**Q: How do I move a marker?**  
A: Tap the lock icon in the marker’s popup to unlock it, drag it to the new location, and it will automatically re-lock when dropped.

**Q: How do I backup my markers?**  
A: Use the “Export Markers” button in the tools panel. You can also choose “Personal + Kept” to include your edited community markers.

**Q: What does the Community Map do?**  
A: It adds verified markers from other players. Tap “Update Community Map” regularly to receive the latest additions.

**Q: The app feels slow or broken.**  
A: Use the “Reset App” button in the How to Use menu, or uninstall the PWA and reinstall it for a clean start.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. All your markers and progress are saved locally on your device only. Nothing is stored on any server.
