# Fallout 76 Item Finder Map

Live version: [https://0mrcrazy0.github.io/fallout76-itemfindermap/](https://0mrcrazy0.github.io/fallout76-itemfindermap/)

A simple, free, no-login browser tool for Fallout 76 players to mark and track items, locations, and discoveries across Appalachia.

## Features

- Right-click anywhere on the map to open the Choose Action menu
- Create permanent markers (automatically locked) or temporary postcards (links expire after 5 minutes)
- Unlock and drag markers to reposition them — they auto-lock when dropped
- Search and filter by description, category, or grid coordinates
- Export and import your markers easily (share with friends)
- Download Community Map to see verified markers from other players
- Earn XP and level up as you explore

## How to Use

1. Open the live map
2. Right-click anywhere to create a marker
3. Use the search bar and tools panel to manage your markers

Enjoy exploring Appalachia!

## FAQ

**Q: How do I create a marker?**  
A: Right-click (or long-press on mobile) anywhere on the map and choose “Create Permanent Marker”.

**Q: What’s the difference between a permanent marker and a postcard?**  
A: Permanent markers stay forever. Postcard links are temporary and expire after 5 minutes — share them with a friend before they disappear.

**Q: Why are some markers glowing green?**  
A: New or recently moved markers glow for 2 minutes.

**Q: How do I move a marker?**  
A: Unlock it first (tap the lock icon), drag it to the new spot, then it will re-lock automatically.

**Q: How do I backup my markers?**  
A: Use the “Export Markers” button in the tools panel.

**Q: What does the Community Map do?**  
A: It adds verified markers submitted by other players. Click “Update Community Map” to get the latest.

**Q: I think my app is not working.**  
A: Reset the app in the How to Use menu, or uninstall the PWA / clear browser cache for a hard reset.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. Everything is saved locally on your device.
